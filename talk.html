Anti-Black Racism

Our struggle is fighting anti-blackness not non-whiteness.

By framing Our struggle and anti-blackness we can see the true enemies wait it's in POC because anyone that falls outside of blackness has the potential to be an enemy not experiencing the same struggle as Our blackness. Race is a physical characteristics associated with human beings. Race is perceived by others based on skin color. Anti-Blackness is a bias towards people of darker skin.

POC Anti-Blackness

POC is term used by non-whites seeking sympathy from black folks. POC does not equal black.

POC is the term used by non-black folks to claim a state of temporary blackness. Especially when they want to either garner sympathy for black folks or to engage in the (minimal) legal protections that we bleed and die for every day.
