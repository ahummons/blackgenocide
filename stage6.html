Preparation

Opinion

Preparation for the cycles of American Genocide have been ongoing. They began with the Jim Crow laws enacted after the Emancipation of slaves in America. “Good Americans” rushed to enact laws to control the education, economic and overall health of African-Americans. Once America was required by law to pay us for work, the plot to terminate our existence began. Racists overlooked that we were forcibly brought here -- to the stolen land now called America. Let’s be very clear that Lincoln only freed the slaves because good ole northern white folks didn’t want slaves brought to the west. The northerners envied the free labor that made the south rich, even if they benefitted from the institution as well. (HYPOCRITS) Jim Crow allowed black people to be penalized for being black. Black men were lucky to be imprisoned when accused of a crime. More often that not they were lynched. Desegregation of schools further infuriated frail white existence. They perceive a threat, but really they were fearful is that black people will enact revenge for the inhumanities done on to us as. As black people fought for civil rights, equal pay, voting rights and fair housing, “Good Americans” were looking for new ways to ensure the American Genocide continued. The war on drugs was used to incarcerate millions of black men at an alarming rate, wile the “Good Americans” pointed fingers about absent fathers. Then the not-so-great Bill Clinton entered office, and enacted some key laws and legislation to promote the genocide – Mandatory minimum, No Federal Aid for Drug Offenders, NAFTA, Welfare Reform. So in a few years he made sure black people would spend 10-15 years in jail for 3 rocks meanwhile Brad gets probation for an ounce of cocaine. Bill can register as a sex offender and go to college when he is released, but Tyrone can’t go to college because he got caught with some weed.
