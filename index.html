The African-American Genocide or Black Genocide

Racism In America

Racism in America or AMERACISM is a disease, but it is also a structure. Racism is institutionalized and enforced through laws and regulation in the United States. The first rule in treating diseases is to identify the pathogens or causes. American society is quick to point externally to the failings and inhumane treatment of people across the globe, wile turning a blind eye to the atrocities still being committed at home. Racism in America continues because the infected pass on the disease to their offspring. They encode education, politics, social behavior, and laws with racism to ensure that it prevails. America blames the victims of oppression on their condition, while taking no responsibility for their creation and efforts to sustain the institution.

Ameracism

AMERACISM : a disease that largely impacts those who suffer from extensive pigmentation of the epidermis. Afflicted individuals symptoms are poverty, substandard education, unjust incarceration, unemployment, high interest rates, medical malpractice, low wages, permanent disenfranchisement, restricted housing, victimization by torture, rape, abuse, homicide and government funded executions.

  • Correlated illnesses: high blood pressure, depression and early death from misdiagnosis/late diagnosis of cancer and other fatal diseases.
  • Infection rate among members of affected population is 98%.
  • Current treatment: none
  • Proposed treatment : complete destruction of social, political and environmental pathogens.
