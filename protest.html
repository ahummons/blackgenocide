The American Genocide

Non-Violent

Protests draw attention to the struggles. The genocide will continue if we ignore it. The genocide is successful when we remain docile and uninformed. Awareness=Awakening

Social Media has allowed a new generation to lead. Young people have always led. We have to take heed to the example set by the Young Generation. They have utilized social media to mobilize and as a means to keep each other aware of issues in the community.

Other Course of Action

We can no longer afford to await major media outlets to change their practice of vilifying and crucifying Black people with no cause. Being a black victim means you must await the media to validate your humanity. We must rely on our news outlets and continue to create our own news outlets. The African American Genocide relies on the media promoting propaganda that results in the bias treatment of African-Americans. The media portrays the narrative of terror to exploit white fear. The white fear is an unfounded fear, because if black people treated white people with a fraction of the hate they traet us with …
the problem would be solved

Bank Black, Shop Black, Educate Black, Entertain Black, Information Black, Love Black because if we don't no one else will.
